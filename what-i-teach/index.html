Mobility Tailored to Your Needs


Over time, I found that little nagging issue would come up in our group classes that I couldn't address for two main reasons: 1) There were just too many people in the room and my students needed more individual attention than I could provide in a group setting 2) It wasn't always clear to me how the highly integrated movements of tai chi and qigong could be broken down to address a specific ache or pain. The methods are certainly there in these systems, but I didn't have the necessary training. So instead, I started training in Z-Health --- now I can provide tailored exercises for specific movement issues.
